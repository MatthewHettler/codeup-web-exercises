National Parks

Arches

  • Arches National Park covers a total area of 76,359 acres.
  • Arches was made a national park on November 12, 1971.
  • The entrance fee for Arches is $30 per vehicle per week.
  • Arches National Park lies in the Mountain time zone.

Denali

  • Denali National Park covers a total area of 6,075,106 acres.
  • Denali was made a national park on February 26, 1917.
  • The entrance fee for Denali is $15 per person per week.
  • Denali National Park lies in the Alaska time zone.

Everglades

  • Everglades National Park covers a total area of 1,507,850 acres.
  • Everglades was made a national park on May 30, 1934.
  • The entrance fee for Everglades is $25 per vehicle per week.
  • Everglades National Park lies in the Eastern time zone.
